Frequently Asked Questions

What are Data Credits (DC)?

DC are non-transferable utility tokens pegged at $0.00001 each. They're used to pay for network access. Think of them like prepaid minutes for WiFi.

Why are DC "soulbound" (non-transferable)?

This prevents speculation and ensures presale participants actually use the network. It keeps AFM focused on real utility, not trading.

How do I become an operator?

Purchase Heavy+ tier during Early Bird presale and commit to deploying a node within 12 months. First 30 spots get FREE equipment financing ($1,500 value).

What if I don't use all my DC?

Unused DC after your emission period expires are donated to the cooperative treasury to support network growth and community programs.

When does the network launch?

Network launch is planned for 4-6 months after presale closes. Exact timing depends on pilot success and operator onboarding.

Can I buy AFM tokens directly?

AFM tokens will be available after network launch. Presale participants receive DC (utility), not AFM (governance). This keeps us focused on building, not speculation.
